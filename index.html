PROJECT 5

Autonomous Water Surface Cleaning Robot

The autonomous system for water surface cleaning enables the quick removal of debris and assures efficient cleaning to maintain environmental sustainability. Materials used in this model are lightweight, like carbon fiber and fiberglass. The proposed structure is based on a hydrodynamic hull design which ensures stability. Propulsion is provided by 500W DC motors equipped with three-blade propellers. Advanced AI models such as YOLO and PPO are used for object detection and navigation. IoT integration enables real-time monitoring, while the collection of debris is done through a conveyor belt system. The paper supports HD cameras with live streaming.
